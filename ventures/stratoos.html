Vision & Mission

StratoOS is the world's first comprehensive transportation operating system designed specifically for the low-altitude economy. We're building the digital infrastructure that will make operating drones, eVTOLs, and autonomous vehicles as intuitive as playing a video game, while maintaining the highest standards of safety and compliance.

The Problem We're Solving

Today's air operations are fragmented, complex, and require specialized training. Pilots and operators must juggle multiple systems, coordinate with air traffic control, and manage complex safety protocols. StratoOS unifies these operations into a single, intuitive platform that democratizes access to the skies.

Key Features

  • BVLOS Support: Beyond Visual Line of Sight operations with advanced autonomy
  • Swarm Intelligence: Multi-vehicle coordination and formation flying
  • Mission Orchestration: Complex task sequencing and resource optimization
  • Safety Certification: Built-in compliance with aviation safety standards
  • Gamified Interface: Intuitive controls inspired by modern gaming

User Experience Revolution

Instead of traditional "pilot" interfaces, StratoOS treats users as "commanders" who issue high-level mission primitives:

  • takeoff() - Automated takeoff with safety checks
  • move_to(lat, lon, alt) - Navigate to specific coordinates
  • orbit(center, radius, duration) - Maintain position in circular pattern
  • land() - Safe landing with obstacle avoidance

Business Model

StratoOS operates on a platform-as-a-service model:

  • OS Licensing: Core operating system licensing for hardware manufacturers
  • SDK & App Store: Developer tools and application marketplace
  • Cloud Services: Airspace management and traffic coordination SaaS
  • Government Partnerships: Municipal and national airspace management contracts

Competitive Advantage

Unlike existing solutions that focus on individual vehicles or specific use cases, StratoOS provides a unified platform that scales from single drone operations to city-wide air traffic management. Our dual-plane architecture ensures both safety and usability, while our open ecosystem enables rapid innovation.
